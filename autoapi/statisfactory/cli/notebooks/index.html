statisfactory.cli.notebooks.build_notebooks(src: pathlib.Path, dst: pathlib.Path)¶

Recursively parse the Notebooks from ‘src’, to extract the Craft’s definition.

Definitions found that way are copied to their .py counterpart. The name of the py file is the name of the notebook with num purged. The functions definitions found in “export” tagged cells, are exported to the root of the package.
